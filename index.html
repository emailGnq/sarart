Buying My Work

All paintings are on stretched canvas, unframed unless stated otherwise. Commissions are on loose canvas for ease of posting. Art media for paintings are as indicated; including acrylic, pencil or mixed media which can include collage.

All paintings are varnished with clear satin finish. Matt or gloss finish is also available for commissioned work.

Commissioned Pet Portraits

Please see examples of the type of painting I can provide, compared to the likeness of the pet involved. These are not ‘hair for hair’ realism but rather paintings that show the character of your pet in a more loose and ‘expressionist’ style.

I can offer a range of sizes of single pet portraits with relative prices. These include the cost of materials and finishing (varnishing) but exclude stretch framing and postage. Pet portraits are posted on loose canvas, ready for mounting.

  A.  16"×16"   £200.00
  B.  20"×20"   £275.00
  C.  24"×24"   £330.00

Please contact me for pricing if you would like more than one pet in any of these three sizes.
